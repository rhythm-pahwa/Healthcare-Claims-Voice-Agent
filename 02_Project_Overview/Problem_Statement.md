# Problem Statement

Healthcare contact centers receive a high volume of calls from members and providers regarding claim status, claim submission, eligibility verification, benefits inquiries, provider lookup, pre authorization requests, and service related issues.

Many of these interactions are handled manually, which can lead to longer wait times, inconsistent responses, increased administrative workload, and delays in resolving user requests.

In addition to operational challenges, healthcare organizations must ensure that sensitive healthcare information is only accessible to authenticated and authorized users while maintaining compliance with HIPAA requirements.

After analyzing the project requirements, we identified the need for a conversational solution capable of securely authenticating users, understanding their requests, routing conversations to the appropriate service flow, and escalating complex scenarios to human agents when necessary.

The challenge is to design a structured and secure Healthcare Claims Voice Agent that can support multiple healthcare journeys while improving efficiency, maintaining compliance, and providing a better user experience for both members and providers.
