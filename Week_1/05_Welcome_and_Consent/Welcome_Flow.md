# Welcome Flow

The welcome flow represents the initial interaction between the caller and the Healthcare Claims Voice Agent.

The purpose of this flow is to greet the caller and initiate the conversation.

## Welcome Conversation

Bot:
Welcome to Healthcare Support.

Bot:
I can assist you with claim status, claim submission, eligibility verification, benefits inquiries, provider lookup, pre authorization status, and service requests.

Bot:
How may I assist you today?

User:
States request.

Bot:
Proceed to Privacy and Consent Flow.

## Flow Summary

- Welcome the caller
- Introduce available services
- Collect initial request
- Continue to Privacy and Consent
