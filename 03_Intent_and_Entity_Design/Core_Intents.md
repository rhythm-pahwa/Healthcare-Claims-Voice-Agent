# Core Intents

After analyzing the healthcare claims use cases, we identified the following core intents that the voice agent should support.

## Claim Status

Allows members or providers to check the current status of a submitted claim.

**Example Utterances:**
- What's my claim status?
- Track my claim.
- Has my claim been approved?
- Check the status of claim CLM10234.
- Where is my claim in the process?

## Claim Submission

Allows users to submit claim related information for processing.

**Example Utterances:**
- I want to submit a claim.
- File a new claim.
- Start a reimbursement claim.
- Submit my healthcare claim.

## Eligibility Check

Allows users to verify healthcare coverage eligibility.

**Example Utterances:**
- Am I eligible for this treatment?
- Check my coverage.
- Is my insurance active?
- Verify my eligibility.

## Benefits Inquiry

Allows users to obtain information regarding healthcare benefits and coverage.

**Example Utterances:**
- What benefits are covered?
- What is my outpatient coverage?
- Tell me about my plan benefits.
- What services are included?

## Provider Lookup

Allows users to search for healthcare providers within the network.

**Example Utterances:**
- Find a doctor near me.
- Search for in-network providers.
- Find a hospital in my area.
- Is this doctor in my network?

## Pre Authorization Status

Allows users to check the status of pre authorization requests.

**Example Utterances:**
- Check my authorization status.
- Has my pre-authorization been approved?
- Do I need pre-authorization?
- What's the status of my authorization request?

## Service Request

Allows users to create service requests for issues requiring additional assistance.

**Example Utterances:**
- Create a support request.
- Open a service ticket.
- Report an issue with my claim.
- I need help with my account.

## Human Agent Transfer

Allows users to connect with a human representative when necessary.

**Example Utterances:**
- Connect me to an agent.
- I want to talk to a representative.
- Transfer me to customer support.
- Let me speak to a human.
