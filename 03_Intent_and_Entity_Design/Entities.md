# Entities

After identifying the supported healthcare journeys, we defined the following entities that may be collected during conversations.

## Member Information

- member_id
- date_of_birth
- zip_code
- address
- ssn_last4

## Claim Information

- claim_id
- claim_amount
- claim_status
- service_date

## Eligibility Information

- plan_type
- coverage_type

## Provider Information

- provider_name
- provider_specialty
- provider_location

## Authorization Information

- authorization_id
- procedure_code

## Service Request Information

- issue_type
- issue_description
- ticket_number
