# Fallback Test Cases

This document validates the fallback mechanism of the Healthcare Claims Voice Agent when the system cannot confidently determine the member's intent. The objective is to recover the conversation through clarification before escalating to a human representative.

| Test Case ID | Test Case Name             | Scenario / Sample Interaction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Expected Behaviour                                                                                                                                                                               | Expected Outcome                                                                                  |
| ------------ | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| FB-001       | Unclear Request            | **User:** I need some help.<br><br>**Agent:** I'd be happy to help. Are you calling about a claim, eligibility, benefits, provider information, or pre-authorization?<br><br>**User:** My claim.<br><br>**Agent:** Thank you. I'll help you with your claim.                                                                                                                                                                         | The agent should recognize that the initial request is incomplete. A clarification question should be asked. The conversation should continue once the intent is identified.                     | The user is routed to the correct claim-related journey after clarification.                      |
| FB-002       | Ambiguous Request          | **User:** I want to know if it's approved.<br><br>**Agent:** Could you please tell me whether you're referring to your claim or your pre-authorization request?<br><br>**User:** My pre-authorization.<br><br>**Agent:** Thank you. Let me check the status of your pre-authorization request.                                                                                                                                       | The agent should avoid making assumptions. The user should be asked to clarify the request. The correct business journey should begin after clarification.                                       | The user is routed to the Pre-Authorization Journey.                                              |
| FB-003       | Multiple Requests          | **User:** I want to check my claim status and also verify my eligibility.<br><br>**Agent:** I can certainly help with both. Let's begin with your claim status first, and then we'll review your eligibility.<br><br>**User:** Okay.                                                                                                                                                                                                 | The agent should detect multiple intents. One request should be completed before starting the next. Conversation context should be maintained.                                                   | The claim status journey starts first, and eligibility is handled next in the same session.       |
| FB-004       | Unsupported Request        | **User:** Can you schedule an appointment with my doctor?<br><br>**Agent:** I'm sorry, appointment scheduling isn't supported. I can help you with claim status, eligibility, benefits, provider search, or pre-authorization. Is there anything related to these services I can assist you with?<br><br>**User:** I'd like to check my eligibility.<br><br>**Agent:** Certainly. Let me help you with your eligibility information. | The agent should explain its supported capabilities. The user should be redirected to a supported journey. The conversation should continue without interruption.                                | The unsupported request is handled safely, and the user is redirected to the Eligibility Journey. |
| FB-005       | Conversation Recovery      | **User:** I want information about my coverage.<br><br>**Agent:** Are you referring to your eligibility or your benefits?<br><br>**User:** My benefits.<br><br>**Agent:** Thank you. I'll retrieve your benefits information.                                                                                                                                                                                                        | The clarification should improve intent confidence. The conversation should resume normally. No Human Escalation should occur.                                                                   | The user is routed to the Benefits Journey after successful clarification.                        |
| FB-006       | Clarification Unsuccessful | **User:** Help.<br><br>**Agent:** I'd be happy to help. Could you tell me what you need assistance with?<br><br>**User:** I don't know.<br><br>**Agent:** Are you calling about a claim, eligibility, provider information, benefits, or pre-authorization?<br><br>**User:** I'm not sure.<br><br>**Agent:** I'm unable to identify your request. I'll connect you with one of our support representatives for further assistance.   | The agent should retry clarification within the configured limit. The conversation should remain polite and professional. Human Escalation should be initiated after the retry limit is reached. | The conversation is transferred to Human Escalation after unsuccessful clarification attempts.    |
